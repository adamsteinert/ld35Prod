Controls:
  • S Change shape
  • F Fire at your enemies
  • ↑ Jump/climb
  • ← Move left
  • → Move right
Hints:
  • When in "green form", scale walls by holding down the direction key toward the wall while pressing up.
  • Shoot at the amorphous red blobs for points!
Bugs:
  • Shifting to "Green form" lets you walk through walls
